What is Scrum

According to Wiki, "Scrum is an Agile process framework for managing complex knowledge work".
Well, that's a fancier way of saying a modern workflow.

Roles

Usually there are three types of roles in scrum.
PRODUCT OWNER
TEAM MEMBERS
SCRUM MASTER

Planning

Which of the items in the BACKLOG are we doing and what is the definition of done.

Sprint

The team works in SPRINTS
A sprint can be somewhere between 2 - 4 weeks, during this time the members complete the list of items.

Standup

STANDUP MEETINGS
Are we going to complete the items on time?
Are we acording to plan?

Review

Deliver FINISHED items after the sprint ends, and recieve feedback.

Retrospective

Look back on the previous sprint, REFLECT and IMPROVE the team.

Repeat

REPEAT all steps until you have your finished product.
